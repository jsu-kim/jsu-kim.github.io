MS/PhD Candidate @ Korea University

I am an MS/PhD candidate at Korea University, advised by Prof. Jongheon Jeong. I work on AI safety for generative models, especially large language models and diffusion models. My research focuses on adversarial robustness, effective use of external knowledge, and mitigating misalignment with human values in rare or unexpected scenarios.
